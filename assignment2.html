About the Greater Antilles

Greater Antilles consists four of the largest islands in the Caribbean. These islands are Cuba, Hispaniola, Jamaica, and Puerto Rico. They constitute nearly 90 percent of the total land area of the entire West Indies. Hispaniola is comprised of two islands, namely Haiti and Dominican Republic.
